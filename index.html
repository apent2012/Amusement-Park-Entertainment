WE ARE NOT AN ADVERTISING AGENCY.

Think of us as a manufacturer of creative content, business models and branded relationships across multiple platforms.

The collective experience of our team is heavily rooted in sports, art, entertainment and emerging culture. From 20+ years of working with dominant brands like Nike, Pepsi and Gatorade to cultivating partnerships with the most influential celebrities, Amusement Park is uniquely positioned to pioneer new territory in an ever-changing industry.

Amusement Park Entertainment is in the business of creating 360º brand experiences through
storytelling and compelling interactive technology.

JIMMY SMITH

Chairman, CEO/CCO

Jimmy has earned countless awards for his work as a Writer and Creative Director for Nike at Wieden + Kennedy, Executive Creative Director at BBDO and most recently, Gatorade’s Group Creative Director at TBWA\Chiat\Day LA. As GCD at TBWA\Chiat\Day, Jimmy oversaw the creation of Gatorade Replay, which gave decades-old, high school sports teams a chance to relive their glory days. Jimmy also spearheaded the transformation of the Gatorade brand to “G,” and was voted as Fast Company’s 100 most creative people in business in 2012.

Say hello at jimmy@amusementparkent.com
